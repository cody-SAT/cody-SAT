What Should I Prioritize?

  1. Complete one practice test before each session. Mark questions you weren’t sure about. Even if you guessed correctly, they are still worth going over
  2. Score your test and have a list of questions you missed/guessed ready for class
  3. Work through Khan Academy practice
  4. Study 1–2 of the English resources each day, or whenever you have time
